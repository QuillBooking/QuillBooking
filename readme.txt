[Quill Booking](https://quillbooking.com/) delivers a comprehensive, professional-grade scheduling solution that streamlines appointment management for businesses of all sizes. == Installation ==

1. Upload the Quill Booking plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Navigate to Quill Booking settings to configure your scheduling preferences
4. Set up your availability and booking parameters
5. Begin accepting appointments through your customized booking interface

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is Quill Booking suitable for enterprise-level organizations? =

Absolutely. Quill Booking is designed with enterprise scalability in mind, offering advanced features, comprehensive integrations, and robust security measures suitable for organizations of all sizes.

= Can I customize the booking experience for my brand? =

Yes, Quill Booking provides extensive customization options including custom forms, branded interfaces, and flexible booking workflows that align with your brand identity.

= Does Quill Booking support multiple team members? =

Yes, our Professional version includes team rotation management, allowing multiple team members to handle bookings with intelligent load balancing and availability management.

= How does payment processing work? =

Quill Booking integrates with leading payment processors including Stripe, PayPal, Google Pay, and Apple Pay, allowing clients to book and pay in a single seamless transaction.

= Is client data secure and compliant? =

Yes, Quill Booking maintains full GDPR compliance with enterprise-grade security protocols to protect client information and maintain regulatory compliance.
